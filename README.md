Ian Szymanski is a designer who specializes in marketing and branding across all mediums. He is the Marketing Coordinator for Big Table, and takes great pride in what he does

Ian solves problems through simple, effective design. He believes in lifting up anyone who isn’t giving their all, inspiring people to be the best forms of themselves. When not creating for others, Ian creates for himself. Playing and arranging musicare both long-time hobbies for Ian. He shares his love of music with others in many ways,  even random comments about music theory to anyone who will listen.
